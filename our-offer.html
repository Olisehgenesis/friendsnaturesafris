Package 1: 4 Days Gorillas, Wildlife Safari and Kazinga Cruise

Lake Mburo National Park is known for Zebras, Kobs, Giraffes and many others animals. Bwindi Impenetrable Forest National Park is entirely covered by dense forest with thick different species of natural vegetation and different melodies of different birds and Bamboo trees which is sweet to the Mountain Gorillas.

  1. DAY 1: KAMPALA TO LAKE MBULO

    You will be escorted by a representative of FRIENDS NATURE SAFARIS at your lodge and drive to Lake Mburo National Park its about 4 hour drive from Kampala-Masaka main road we shall have a stopover at the Equator for photo shots then continue with our journey to Lake Mburo.Upon arrival we check in at our lodge and then have lunch after we do a boat cruise on Lake Mburo when back have evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay at Mihingo Safari Lodge (Any Equivalent)

  2. DAY 2: LAKE MBULO – BWINDI IMPENETRABLE NATIONAL PARK

    Early morning we shall do safari walk around the park to see animals like Giraffes, Kobs, Zebras and many more, breakfast then leave for Bwind Impenetrable National Park .We shall drive via a scenic route, savanna vegetation and Ankole cattle and precede our drive through the green growth countryside, we shall have lunch on the way and final we arrive in Bwindi and check in at our Lodge for dinner and overnight at Silver Back Lodge Luxury (Any Equivalent).

  3. DAY 3: BWINDI IMPENETRABLE FOREST NATIONAL PARK

    In the morning we take our breakfast and assemble for briefing according to the group which we are going to trek/track about the does and donots.This activity is one of highlight of our safari, meeting with the gentle giants of Bwindi, Especially hikers will love the gorilla trekking in the beautiful ecosystem of the park. It is difficult to foresee how many hours you will hike, it may take two to eight hours. Expect to walk a long distance in steep and muddy conditions, sometimes with rain overhead, before you encounter any gorillas. But we found these creatures it is very rewarding and interesting as you observe them closely while they eat, play and rest. A unique and unforgettable experience! For conservation purposes, time spent with the gorillas is limited to one hour. The ranger guide always briefs you on how to behave with these giant apes. After the trek we return back to lodge for lunch and evening at leisure. Dinner and overnight at Silver Back Lodge Luxury (Any Equivalent).

  4. DAY 4: BWINDI IMPENETRABLE TO KAMPALA

    Warm wake up and then we take breakfast, we pack our belongs and embark on our Safari van and leave the park for Kampala, we shall have lunch on the way and a stopover at Mpambire Drum Makers to buy souvenir to take home. Finally we arrive in Kampala City.

Package 2: Day 1: Entebbe Airport –Ngamba Island.

  1. Day 1: Kampala to Murchsion Falls National Park.

    Depending on the time you arrive you meet our guide at Entebbe International Airport and transfer to Entebbe Backpackers check in, then visit Ngamba Island for orphaned Chimpanzees after that return back to the Campsite for dinner and overnight at full board basis ,If you arrive late you spent a night in kampala at Natete Backpackers. • Driving time: ± 3 hours • Entebbe /Kampala Backpackers.

  2. Day 2: Entebbe/Kampala to Kibale Forest National Park.

    Early morning we will be picked up and driven by our guide/drive to Fort Portal district via Mubende. Making a stopover at Nakayima tree shrine believed to be hosting the spirit of Nakayima.we will have a lunch break before touching down late afternoon in Fort Portal. We continue to the park and check in at our lodge for Dinner and overnight stay. • Driving time: ± 6,5 hours • Camp site

  3. Day 3: Kibale Forest National Park.

    This day you will start searching for Chimpanzees and other primates including red tailed and grey cheek Mangabey and other primates, the guide will provide detailed explanations not only on primates but also the forest fauna and flora and will ensure that your walk is both informative and enjoyable. Afternoon visit to Bigodi. This walk is excellent for viewing bird life. • Camp site

  4. Day 4: Kibale Forest NP To Queen Elizabeth NP.

    After break fast ,Proceed to queen Elizabeth National park following the mystical Rwenzori Mt. Range , a game drive en route to view Lion, Elephant, Leopard, Buffalo, Hyena and a variety of Antelope species. Enjoy the unique 2 hour boat ride on the Kazinga Channel. You will have a chance to view Hippos on close range and other bird species Dinner and overnight at Mweya Campsite

  5. Day 5: Queen Elizabeth National Park.

    After break fast ,Proceed to queen Elizabeth National park following the mystical Rwenzori Mt. Range , a game drive en route to view Lion, Elephant, Leopard, Buffalo, Hyena and a variety of Antelope species. Enjoy the unique 2 hour boat ride on the Kazinga Channel. You will have a chance to view Hippos on close range and other bird species Dinner and overnight at Mweya Campsite

  6. Day 6: Queen Elizabeth NP to L.Mburo NP

    Leave Queen Elizabeth to Lake Mburo National park with a stop over at Mbarara town for lunch. After lunch, proceed to the park with game drive en-route. Evening, nature walk in the savannah park is amazing you should also know that Lake Mburo is the only park in Uganda where you can carry out a safari on foot so many animals can be spotted with accompany of an armed guide. Package 3: 8 Days MFNP, KIBALE, QENP and BWINDI

  1. Day 1: Kampala to Murchsion Falls National Park.

    Depart for Murchison Falls National Park in the north southern part of Uganda and is the largest park with an area of 3840SQKM protecting a chunck of untamed savannah bisected by the mighty River Nile this journey will take approximately 5-6 hours on road having an en-route lunch at masindi town and then we continue to the park check in at Red Chilli Rest Campsite for Dinner and over night stay

  2. Days 2: Game Drive & Boat Cruise.

    An early morning wake up call with packed breakfast go for the thrilling game drive where you can spot as many animals as you can like Elephants, Buffalo, Giraffe ,Lions Uganda kobs , variety of Antelopes and many others if you lucky you can get a chance to see the world’s most rare Shoebill Stork at the down side of the stream during the game drive retire at the camp rest and have lunch as you preparing for the interesting lunch cruise in the afternoon where you will spot animals like Hippos, Crocodiles populations, Elephants and other animals at the side of the River drinking water the boat will stop at the actual point where you can see clearly the mighty Nile and take photos, Dinner and over night stay Red Chilli Rest Campsite

  3. Day 3: Leave for Kibale Forest Np

    In the morning after taking breakfast we go to the top of the falls where the mighty Nile is being squeezed into a 7 meter cervical and reach apoint where it rains every time, spend some time here and after we proceed to Kibale Forest National Park which was created in 1993 and has emerged the best destination for chimpanzee tracking in Uganda,it is also popular for 12 other species of primates,you arrive late and check in at CRATER VALLY CAMPSITE for dinner and overnight stay .

  4. DAY 4: Tracking Chimps

    This day you will enter the Forest searching for Chimpanzees and other primates including red tailed and grey cheek Mangabey and other primates, the guide will provide detailed explanations not only on primates but also the forest fauna and flora and will ensure that your walk is both informative and enjoyable, Return to the camp for lunch and proceed to Queen Elizabeth National Park, arrive late in the evening and check in at Mwaya Campsite For dinner and overnight stay.

  5. Day 5: Game Drive & Kazinga Channel Cruise.

    Early morning, wake up take a cup of tea and head for the thrilling game drive at 6:00am game to view Lion, Elephant, Leopard, Buffalo, Hyena and a variety of Antelope species,In the afternoon after lunch you enjoy the unique 2 hour boat ride on the Kazinga Channel. You will have a chance to view Hippos on close range and other bird species; you retire for dinner and overnight at Mweya Campsite

  6. Day 6: Queen Elizabeth to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest

    After breakfast you leave Queen Elizabeth National park and head to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park passing through green grassland having en-route lunch on the way to the park arrive check in at Buhoma community campsite For dinner and overnight stay.

  7. Day 7: Gorilla Tracking.

    After early breakfast get ready to start up with search an interesting activity Gorilla Tracking which is the highlight of the safari ,you can move long distances in the muddy area before you see the world’s rare gentle giants(Mountain Gorillas) Spend time watching the gorillas go about their activities, Each encounters differently and has its own rewards, but you are likely to enjoy the close view of adults feeding, grooming and resting as the youngsters frolic and swing from vines in a delightfully playful display as your taking pictures with no flash on the camera. Return to the camp having evening at leisure or you can opt to go for a community walk Dinner and overnight stay at Buhoma community campsite

  8. Day 8: Bwindi to Kampala

    After relaxed breakfast say bye to land of the pygmies and head to kampala having en-route lunch along the way and a stopover at the Ugandan Equator where you can take photos and shop Ugandan crafts.
